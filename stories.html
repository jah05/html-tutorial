Ravens Cut Pro-Bowl Safety Earl Thomas

8/23/2020 4:46PM Nick

Following an altercation between Pro-Bowl Safety Earl Thomas and teammate Chuck Clark, the Ravens have decided to cut Thomas who was expected to be paid $10 million.

After receiving news of his termination, the safety posted on his instagram: Had a great run .. Wish things would have ended different but you live and you learn. Thank you [Ravens GM] Eric DeCosta and everyone else who played a role in bringing him to B-More. Wish you guys the best.
